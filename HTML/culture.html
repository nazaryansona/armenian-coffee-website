Armenian Coffee Culture ☕

Coffee is not just a drink in Armenia — it is part of everyday life.

☀️ Morning Ritual

Many Armenians start their day with coffee. It is a daily ritual that helps people wake up and prepare for the day.

👥 Coffee in every situation

Coffee is often shared with friends, neighbors, and family. It is the basic way to spend time together. You can drink coffee any time and anywhere.

💬 Conversations & Gossip

Drinking coffee is strongly connected with conversations. People gather, talk, and sometimes gossip while enjoying coffee.

🔮 Coffee Reading

One unique tradition is reading coffee grounds. After drinking, people interpret the patterns left in the cup.

🚬 Coffee and Cigarette

This combo is so popular especially among men and young people. It is also part of a daily routine. If coffee then with cigarette and if cigarette then with coffee.
